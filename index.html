Common Questions

Everything You Need
to Know

Answers to the most common questions about Fidex Paradise, our services, and how to get started.

Are you CIDB registered? +
Yes. Fidex Paradise Manufacturing and Projects is fully registered with the Construction Industry Development Board (CIDB) at GB Grade 1. This means we meet all South African regulatory requirements for construction contractors, giving our clients full peace of mind and legal protection on every project.
Do you work in Katlehong and surrounding areas? +
Yes. We are based in Katlehong East and proudly serve the wider Johannesburg East, Ekurhuleni, and Gauteng region — including Germiston, Boksburg, Alberton, Vosloorus, Springs, Benoni, and surrounding townships and suburbs.
How fast can I get a quote? +
We aim to respond to all quote requests within 2 hours during business hours (Monday–Friday 07:00–17:00, Saturday 08:00–13:00). For urgent requirements, contact us directly via WhatsApp for the fastest response.
Do you offer property maintenance contracts? +
Yes. We offer monthly and annual property maintenance contracts for residential complexes, commercial buildings, and rental properties. Contact us to discuss a customised maintenance plan for your property.
What areas of construction do you cover? +
We offer general building and construction, painting (interior and exterior), roofing installation and repairs, plastering and wall finishing, cleaning services (residential and commercial), landscaping and outdoor design, handyman services, and property maintenance contracts.
Do you provide written quotes? +
Yes. All our quotes are provided in writing with a full breakdown of materials, labour, and timeline. We believe in full transparency — no hidden costs, no surprises when the job is done.
